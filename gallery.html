SanSan Messaging Grid in play

Easily search for cards. Let people know what you're talking about.

SanSan Messaging Grid brings card stats, text and even a thumbnail directly to your Slack channel.

View decklists, directly in Slack. Easily pull that decklist you're thinking about bringing on Saturday into your chat.

SanSan Messaging Grid is fully aware of the NAPD Most Wanted List, and can calculate the influence usage of Alliance cards in decklists.

Make inline searches for cards in your messages. Don't interrupt the conversation to refer to a card.

SanSan Messaging Grid uses a fuzzy text matching algorithm to search, and as a last resort will attempt to automatically match acronyms.
